Membership Requirements

To be a "Member" of SSAR, a person must fill out and turn in a membership application every 12 months.

To be “Field Certified” and placed on the Call-Out list a person must:

- Pass an individual equipment check

- Meet Training Requirements

- Become New Mexico Field Certified within 12 months of being placed on the callout list

- Complete ICS 100 within 3 Months of being placed onto the Call Out List

- Complete ICS 200 within 3 Months of being placed onto the Call Out List

- Actively attend meetings and trainings
